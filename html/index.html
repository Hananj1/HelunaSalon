Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel or reschedule my appointment? +

You can easily manage your bookings through the "My Appointments" page. Simply find your appointment and click on "Edit" to reschedule or "Cancel" to remove it.

What is the salon's cancellation policy? +

We kindly ask for at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations. This allows us to offer the slot to other clients on our waiting list.

Do you accept walk-ins? +

While we love seeing you, we highly recommend booking in advance through our website to ensure we can dedicate the perfect amount of time to your service.
